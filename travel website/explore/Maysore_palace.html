Overview

The official residence of the erstwhile royal family of Mysore, Mysore Palace is an architectural masterpiece showcasing a blend of Hindu, Muslim, Rajput and Gothic styles.

What To Expect?

The palace houses an impressive collection of royal antiques and artefacts. The exterior looks especially striking during Dussehra when it is illuminated with over 95,000 lightbulbs.

Tips

  • The palace is also illuminated on Sundays and public holidays between 7:00 p.m. and 8:00 p.m.
  • Visitors need to remove their shoes to enter the palace.
  • There is a Sound and Light show at the palace from Mondays to Saturdays.
