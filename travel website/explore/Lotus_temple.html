Overview

The Lotus Temple is an edifice of Bahai faith, resembling a lotus flower. It’s constructed purely with white marble and designed by Furiburz Sabha. The temple is open to people of all faiths.

What To Expect?

Explore the green landscape of the temple along with the nine water pools, lined with each petal that glimmer in daylight. You can also meditate inside the payer hall.

Tips

  • The nearest metro station is Kalkaji Mandir.
  • The temple opens between 9:00 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. on all days except Monday.
  • Permission is required for photography.
  • Do explore the Lotus Temple when it’s flood lit at dusk.
  • Silence is compulsory in some areas so pay attention to instruction signs.
